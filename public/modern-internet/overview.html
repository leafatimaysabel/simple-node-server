Overview of the Modern Internet


Today's Internet is regularly evolving. It contains enormous amounts of information. Aside from providing us amazing information, interacting with others are easy as pie. Communication is better, faster and more stable.


Today, the internet connects millions of computers to one another. By 2012, the number of Internet users worldwide reached 2.4 billion - about one-third of the world's population.
